How to read the corridor

Four states. Clear actions.

Advuman translates corridor pressure into four operating states, each with a clear response posture.

ACTIVE
Material risk. Act now.
Multiple pressure layers are elevated and disruption risk is no longer theoretical. This is the point where operators should review open exposure and prepare to act.
→ Review exposure now
WATCH
Signal detected. Monitor.
Pressure is building, but disruption is not yet fully compounded. Operators should increase monitoring and prepare basic contingencies.
→ Increase monitoring
STABLE
Normal conditions. Continue.
No unusual pressure is building across the corridor. Signals remain within expected operating range.
→ Continue normal operations
RECOVERY
Pressure easing. Stay alert.
The corridor is de-escalating, but not fully back to baseline. This can be a useful window to renegotiate or reset terms before conditions normalise fully.
→ Use the recovery window
